# SKT SK Hyper AI data-center business-development subsidiary

## Verified source facts
- AI타임스 input time: 2026-07-24 08:16 KST; SK텔레콤 board approval: 2026-07-23
- SKT established 100%-owned SK Hyper and announced a total investment limit of KRW 750 billion through 2030, funded in installments as needed
- The company assigns site acquisition, substation construction and operation, customer acquisition, and commercialization to the subsidiary
- Reported first-stage target: phased opening of 5 GW AI data centers by 2029; longer-term business-development target: 15 GW
- The report names Ulsan, Chungcheong, and southwest regions for GW-scale plans
